Totally Historically Accurate D-Day Simulator (THADDaySim)

This is a game that I made with Godot - it's themed around the Allied landings at Normandy during D-Day so there are two sides: Allies and Axis. The Allies are trying to get to the end of the map and capture the Axis flag while the Axis are trying to stop them - the game is meant to be played with two players. It's kind of like a "digital board game" in a way.

You can browse the source code on GitHub.

You can read more about this game on my blog.
