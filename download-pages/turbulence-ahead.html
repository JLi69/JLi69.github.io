Turbulence Ahead!

A simple 2D flying simulator game with extreme weather that I made for the 2022 One Lone Coder Codejam. It's a simple game, just fly to the cities that you have to fly to as guided by an arrow before running out of fuel or running into dangerous weather.

It doesn't have the most exciting gameplay but it is quite relaxing to play.

Controls/Important Notes:
- Left/Right Arrows: Rotate/turn plane
- Up/Down Arrows: Speed up/slow down plane
- Escape: Pause game
- Keep an eye on your fuel level!
